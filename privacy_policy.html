Privacy Policy for Ascence Cleaning Services

At Ascence Cleaning Services, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose personal information from our customers and website visitors.

1. Collection of Personal Information
We collect personal information from our customers and website visitors to provide our cleaning services and to improve our website. The personal information we collect may include:
- Name, address, and contact information
- Payment information
- Cleaning preferences and instructions
- Information about the properties we clean
- Feedback and reviews

We collect personal information through various methods, including:
- Contact forms on our website
- Phone and email communication
- In-person meetings
- Third-party payment processors

2. Use of Personal Information
We use personal information to provide our cleaning services and to improve our website. Specifically, we use personal information to:
- Schedule and perform cleaning services
- Process payments
- Communicate with our customers
- Respond to inquiries and requests
- Improve our website and services
- Send promotional emails and newsletters (with consent)
3. Disclosure of Personal Information
We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. However, we may disclose personal information to third parties in the following circumstances:
- To our cleaning staff to provide cleaning services
- To third-party payment processors to process payments
- To regulatory bodies and law enforcement agencies in compliance with legal obligations
- To third-party service providers who help us operate our business and improve our services
4. Protection of Personal Information
We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse. We use secure servers and encryption technologies to protect personal information. We also limit access to personal information to authorized employees and contractors who need to know the information to perform their jobs.
5. Retention of Personal Information
We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our cleaning services and to comply with legal obligations. We may also retain personal information for a reasonable period after the end of our relationship with a customer to maintain business records and to resolve disputes.
6. Access to and Correction of Personal Information
Customers have the right to access and correct their personal information held by us. To request access or correction, customers should contact us using the contact information provided below.
7. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify customers of any material changes to this Privacy Policy by posting a notice on our website or by other means. Customers are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
